Story Concept - Nehmen

A narrative driven horror game which focuses on the themes of helplessness and cosmic horror.

Characters

Nehmen

Nehmen is the main antagonist and catalyst for the game. He exists as a 4 dimensional creature, traversing through different 3D planes as he moves towards dead people’s lingering souls like a moth towards a flame.

  • Nehmen’s 4d body takes the shape of a humanoid angel, but since he can only be seen in 3d he is incomprehensible and abstract.
  • The Angels that people think they saw throughout the Bible were just different parts of Nehmen drifting through their plane.
  • Nehmen’s body is not physical, it exists on the same realm that souls do. This means that he can’t interact with physical things and he can only interact with souls.
  • When Nehmen passes through a person, he drags their soul along with him through different 3 dimensional planes. This causes a hellish eternity for any souls he takes. (Interdimensional eternal roller coaster) If you’re a human and you watch Nehmen take someone’s soul, it will appear as if they just dropped dead for no reason.
  • Nehmen’s followers warp their ideas about him with their own Christian beliefs. They believe that he lives in heaven and when he takes someone’s soul, he is taking it to heaven with him.
  • Nehmen is attracted to large clusters of souls. Usually after death, souls fade out, however because the cult killed themselves in a ritualistic way, their souls remain, leaving a giant cluster which Nehmen is attracted to after they are released from the construction site.
  • By the souls of the Spaniards and Natives being trapped in one place, it’s creating a hot-spot to attract Nehmen. Thus risking the lives of the people on campus as he gets closer to them.

(Taking stats, english comp, us gov, journalism) He begins the story believing that the world is hopeless and no matter what he does, he cannot change anything.

  • Ivan’s main weapon is an Antique Spanish pistol. this pistol came from the original cult-leader, when his brother dropped it after being taken by Nehmen
  • He has a cat that he feeds every day, but ends up loses interest/forgetting to feed the cat, causing it to disappear eventually.
  • Hanging out with josh, sees a guy harassing woman, they decide to step on but Ivan takes it too far and beats the hell out of the guy. Gameplay, you keep pressing a button to beat up the guy and you can’t stop till he’s hurt
    • Ivan has a sister that has been sexually assaulted before and so that’s also part of the reason Ivan beats the living shit out of the dude.
  • Freshmen can’t bring their cars, Ivan takes rides from other people

  • By the end of the game, he has lost all morals and acts at random.

Plot Devices

The Human Soul

The soul makes up a person’s empathy and morality. When Nehmen breaks down a person’s soul, they lose their morality.

  • People with strong morals have solid shaped souls, people with weak morals have more dispersed and weak souls. Nehmen’s passive effect on people turns their souls weak and dispersed. This is a natural function of his body and it’s purpose is to make each soul cling to his body better.
The Cult-Leader’s Gun

On the day that the Spanish Cult-Leader learned about Nehmen, it was because while he and his brother were speaking, Nehmen passed through their home. The cult-leader’s brother used his pistol to shoot at Nehmen, but Nehmen simply passed through him, killing him instantly. Since Nehmen passed through the gun, it was fused with his brother’s soul and it gave it special properties allowing it to shoot through souls and kill ghosts.

Story (Lore)

Marked by year > month. Bullet Points are in Chronological Order.

Reference

Lore

In the year 1550, a Spanish man begins to have recurring dreams of a colossal deity named Nehmen. One day he witnessed Nehmen kill his brother, and Nehmen drove his soul mad, prompting him to start a cult. He gains a following within Spain and is eventually excommunicated by the catholic church. With nowhere else to go, he and his followers flee to the Florida Everglades. While in Florida, his followers start to get the dreams as well. Slowly, their minds get corrupted and they get more violent. The dreams tell them that on the final day, they should all sacrifice themselves to Nehmen, and that he would take them to paradise. In a twisted justification, they decide to “enlighten” the nearby Seminole tribe as well, leading to a bloody battle, that the Spaniards came out on top of. Just before the final day, the remaining Spaniards drag the bodies to a cave and seal themselves to rote.

Plot

Introduction

When Ivan transfers to Southern Seminole University partway through the fall semester, things seem weird. The air smells metallic, cries of war echo in the halls. The strangest part; no one seems to think anything of it. During his first week, a senior janitor is inexplicably found dead. He witnesses the aftermath and despite the horrific scene, the death is ruled a suicide. Ivan being a journalist, he decides to investigate further. He learns that over the past few months, people at the university have become more aggressive and violent. It seems like the janitor died of something he was exposed to. Something the rest of the students still currently being exposed to.

Final Scene

Nehmen takes Ivan’s soul along with the rest of the college. Millions of years pass while they are dragged through space, eventually their souls are rubbed off, one by one, where they are reborn into new bodies, like pollen spread to a new flower by a bee.
